Privacy Policy

Data Protection at https://wortkraftmaster.com/

Personal Data

Personal data is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual (e.g., name, address, phone number, email address, etc.).

Data We Collect

Access to the website https://wortkraftmaster.com/ is free and does not require registration. When contacting us via contact forms or email, you provide your data (contact details, name, and information about yourself in the message text). This data is only accessible to Wortkraftmaster employees to whom it is addressed for the purpose of resolving your inquiry.

Providing your personal data is voluntary. We do not process more personal data than is necessary to respond to your inquiry.

Protection of Your Personal Data

Wortkraftmaster respects your privacy and processes your personal data fairly and lawfully.

We store your personal data in electronic form and apply appropriate technical, organizational, and personnel measures to protect personal data and procedures to prevent unauthorized access to personal data.

Electronic Messages

When you send us email with your personal data that can identify you, whether it is an email with a question or through contact forms located on the website https://wortkraftmaster.com/ that you submit to us by email, we use this data only for the purpose of fulfilling your inquiries.

Deletion and Your Access to Personal Data

We will keep your personal data that you have made available to us for the duration of the existence of the website https://wortkraftmaster.com/. You are authorized at any time to request notification about which personal data we have in our email database, as well as to request that all or some personal data be changed or deleted. You can do this by sending a notification with your request to our Management Board via the contact address info@wortkraftmaster.com. If you make such a request, we will make reasonable efforts to verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access to personal data.

Sharing Personal Data with Other Recipients

We do not share your personal data with other recipients. However, on the website https://wortkraftmaster.com/ you will find links to other websites. Privacy policies on those portals may be different from the Privacy Policy on https://wortkraftmaster.com/. On our site, we may also offer you the use of Wortkraftmaster's social networks (Facebook, Google Plus) that allow sharing of personal data. Therefore, please familiarize yourself with the Privacy Policies that apply to other websites and social networks.

Limitation of Liability

Although we take available technical, organizational, and personnel measures to prevent unauthorized disclosure of your personal data, we cannot guarantee that some of the personal data we collect will never be accidentally disclosed, contrary to the provisions of this Privacy Policy. To the maximum extent permitted by law, we exclude liability for damage caused to users or third parties by accidental disclosure of personal data.

Server Statistics

This website uses Google Analytics to measure visitor statistics.

Cookies

We reserve the right to use cookies on the website https://wortkraftmaster.com/. Cookies are a set of data that serve as your anonymous individual identifier, in such a way that an individual page on the website https://wortkraftmaster.com/ that you visit sends it to your browser.

When you access an individual page https://wortkraftmaster.com/, we send you cookies that are stored on your computer and serve to record information about your previous visits (e.g., how many times you have accessed the pages https://wortkraftmaster.com/). This means that during each subsequent access to https://wortkraftmaster.com/ we will look for your cookie to read the data recorded in it. The cookie does not contain your personal data but enables faster and more efficient activation of information, data, and settings that you have already previously communicated when accessing and using https://wortkraftmaster.com/. Our cookies on your computer cannot be accessed by any website that is not part of the https://wortkraftmaster.com/ website.

https://wortkraftmaster.com/ tracks statistical visits to its pages solely for the purpose of obtaining necessary information about the readability of its pages and uses a third-party service called Google Analytics. Detailed third-party information about this service, as well as users' options to regulate cookie settings that are necessary for this, are available at:

http://www.google.com/analytics/learn/privacy.html

By disabling cookies, you decide whether you want to allow cookies to be stored on your computer. Cookie settings can be controlled and configured in your internet browser settings.
